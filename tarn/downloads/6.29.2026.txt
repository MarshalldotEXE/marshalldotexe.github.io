New Content:

- Cavern levels may now contain caged items, special altars, or monsters and their respective keyholders
- Many more types of keyholders and vestibules have been added:
  - Burning grass rooms
  - Piranha/salamander trap rooms
  - Caustic gas trap rooms
  - Sacrificial altar rooms
  - Electric crystal rooms
  - Pink/purple/red crystal rooms
  - Crumbling floor rooms
  - Barricaded areas
  - Paralysis trap areas
  - Dewar trap areas
  - Thieving monster areas
- Three new melee weapons:
  - Kyoketsu-shoge:      middle-tier dagger, +3 range and sneak attack bonus
  - Kusarigama:          middle-tier scythe, +3 range but no arc slash
  - Three-headed flails: middle-tier bludgeon, auto-attacks when moving and hits three times but attacks very slowly
- Curse enchantments have been added:
  - Weapons of aggravation, infection, mercy, plenty, vanishing, polymorphism, and teleportation
  - Armor of immolation, hypothermia, electrocution, crystallization, pestilence, repulsion, and displacement
  - Slaying enchantments for armor
  - Immunity enchantments for weapons
- Two new enemies
- Two new gamemodes:
  - Explore Mode: Take 1/5 as much damage (score is not saved)
  - Wizard Mode: Take no damage and start with powerful items (score is not saved)


UI Changes:

- Saved games now show the class, level, depth, turn number, and game mode
- An Affinities button now shows in the bottom-left corner for non-Deprived characters
- When hovering over giant monsters, all of their glyphs are now highlighted
- Empty levels composed entirely of rock are now specifically called "Level Boundaries"
- Throwing clubs have been renamed to throwing hammers
- Rewritten some monster descriptions and wrote descriptions for monsters that previously had none
- Rewritten some misleading/obsolete weapon descriptions


Gameplay Changes:

- Level generation has been made more efficient and less buggy
- The first level now always contains a vault
- Brick walls and statues appear in more rooms
- Some keyholders and vestibules no longer spawn in cold or dry biomes
- Turret vestibules and keyholders contain stronger turrets
- Entrances to depth 13 and depth 26 no longer appear next to each other
- The initial level of depth 13 has been given a makeover
- The downstairs on depth 13 appear much more frequently
- The walls of depth 26 now change periodically
- The upstairs and downstairs on depths 32-34 appear slightly less frequently
- A few depths are now more populated with torches, statues, and flavor machines
- Liquid generation per depth has been adjusted
- Item generation per depth has been adjusted
- Monster generation per depth has been adjusted
- The two final affinities in the Smithing tree now instead allow you to craft enchanted items (the removed affinities will be moved to the Tinkering tree in a later update)
- Magician conducts are no longer failed if you can't see the monster
- Electrified weapons no longer scale with upgrades
- Illusory slip now only reduces damage to 5
- Incendiary darts now spawn a huge flash of light for one turn
- Throwing weapons can now spawn upgraded or enchanted
- Upgraded throwing weapons have less of a chance to break
- Adjusted the varieties of slaying/immunity enchantments
- Forest drakes have been replaced with griffins to better fit the animal forest theme


Bugfixes:

- Fixed carried items and buffs not loading from saves
- Fixed swapper keyholders spawning too often
- Fixed the upstairs on depth 14 not working in certain cases
- Fixed a crash related to monsters casting bursts of thunder
- Fixed a crash related to casting spells of regrowth on depth 26
- Fixed many cases of monsters' names not being randomized while hallucinating
- Fixed elf arachnomancers not spawning spiders
