Biomes

Crystal caverns, frozen ravines, overgrown labyrinths, and vast seas of lava encompass the crust of this earth, teaming with natural wildlife, tribal warriors, vengeful undead, and eldritch abominations.

Equipment

Scattered beneath the earth are a variety of magical weapons, scrolls, potions, and other artifacts of which your character and their enemies can use.

Affinities

Every time your character levels up, they gain one affinity point, which can be applied to any one of their class's four unique affinity trees to enhance their proficiencies with weapons, magic, or other abilities.
