TARN

TARN is a traditional roguelike game being actively developed as a passion project.

Your goal is to retrieve the mythical Amulet of Yendor in the bowels of the Fog of War and return it to Death, who bides their time far above the surface of the earth.

Biomes

You will embark across endless world generation. Crystal caverns, frozen ravines, overgrown labyrinths, and vast seas of lava encompass the crust of this earth. Towers, temples, and other dungeons containing special treasures reside deep within these subterranean caverns and overarching skies.

Equipment

You can master a variety of weapons, cast from a grimoire of spells, cook up all kinds of disasters with alchemy, and discover ancient artifacts to further your character's destruction on the world.

Monsters

These caverns are teaming with natural wildlife, tribal warriors, vengeful undead, eldritch abominations, and followers of dieties, outfitted with all kinds of harmful effects of which you will have to deal with constantly.

Classes

You play as a member of one of seven character classes. Each class starts the game with some basic equipment and has access to four unique affinity trees.

Affinities

Every time you level up, you gain one affinity point, which can be applied to any one of your character's affinity trees to enhance their proficiencies with weapons, magic, or other abilities.
