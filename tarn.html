TARN is a traditional roguelike game being actively developed as a passion project.

Your goal is to retrieve the mythical Amulet of Yendor in the bowels of the Fog of War and return with it alive to the surface.

How to Play

Move around the dungeon with the mouse, the vi-keys, or keypad. You can also examine features in the dungeon by hovering over them with the mouse or by pressing 'Enter' and moving the cursor. Other commands are listed in the help menu, accessed by pressing '?'.

Your goal is to reach depth 26, obtain the Amulet of Yendor, and ascend the upstairs on depth 1 -- all without dying in the process.

Tips

Scrolls of upgrade are found scattered throughout the dungeon. When you read it, it will allow you to permanently make a single item more powerful. Weapons will deal more damage and hit more accurately; armor will provide more defense and let you dodge better; wands and charms will increase in effect and recharge rate; rings will increase in effect; relics will be recharged a noticeable amount.

Food items are also found scattered throughout the dungeon. You have a limited amount of nutrition before you begin to starve, so it'll be necessary that you keep exploring to find more food.

Most items are found unidentified -- their magical properties will be hidden unless they are used in some way or discovered through other means. Cursed equipment cannot be unequipped unless the curse is broken by a scroll of uncurse, upgrade, or blessing.

Attacking a monster whom is either paralyzed or has not yet noticed you will result in a sneak attack, which deals triple damage and never misses.

For the truly skillful, there exist depths below 26 with unique challenges. Also, achieving a Feat will increase your final score by 10,000 points, and winning the game while succeeding in a Conduct will increase your final score by 50,000 points. You can view your Feats and Conducts progress by pressing 'F'.
